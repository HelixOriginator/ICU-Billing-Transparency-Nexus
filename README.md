ICU Billing Transparency Nexus

A crowdsourced, research-driven platform designed to bring greater transparency to ICU billing practices and end-of-life care costs.

Overview

Healthcare decisions in critical care settings are often made under extreme emotional and time pressure. This project aims to provide a structured, anonymized view of ICU billing data to help identify patterns in medical costs during the final stages of life.

By aggregating real-world data, the platform enables patients, families, and researchers to better understand how medical expenses evolve in critical care scenarios.

Key Features
Anonymous Data Submission
Families can securely upload ICU bills without revealing personal identity.
AI-Based Pattern Analysis
Detects trends in high-cost interventions, especially during the last 24–48 hours of care.
Data Visualization (Heat Maps)
Highlights cost concentration patterns across hospitals and regions.
Transparency Metrics
Generates aggregated insights to encourage accountability and informed decision-making.
Purpose

This project is built to:

Promote transparency in healthcare billing
Support independent research on end-of-life care
Empower patients and families with better information
Encourage ethical and evidence-based medical practices
How It Works
Users upload anonymized ICU billing documents
AI models analyze cost patterns and treatment timelines
Data is aggregated and visualized into public insights
Trends are shared without exposing individual identities
Ethical Approach
No personally identifiable information is collected or displayed
Data is used only in aggregated and anonymized formats
The platform is intended for awareness, not accusation
